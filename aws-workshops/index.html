Learning with AWS Workshops

2022-02-03 | aws workshops

For learning a new AWS Technology you can read the manual, find some Youtube video or do a course. But a relative unknown source is the AWS Workshop. Before these workshops were mostly exclusive to join during Re:invent in Las Vegas, but the workshops team at aws is collection and sharing them.

There are over 100 workshops on all kinds of topics and levels.

The AWS Levels are defined as following:

  • 100 Beginner
  • 200 Intermediate
  • 300 Advanced
  • 400 Expert

Well Architected

The Well Architected Framework is the important base for any AWS workload. In consists of 6 (Since Re:invent 2021) Pillars:

  • Operational Excellence
  • Security
  • Reliability
  • Performance Efficiency
  • Cost Optimization
  • Sustainability

The workshop at https://www.wellarchitectedlabs.com/ gives a great introduction and deep dive in topics around the framework.
