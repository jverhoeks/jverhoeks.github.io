About Me

Data & AI Technology Lead, AWS Cloud Architect, and Tech Enthusiast

Hello, I'm Jacob 👋

I'm a Data & AI Technology Lead based in The Netherlands, working at Schuberg Philis.

I lead data and AI initiatives, bringing together cloud architecture, machine learning, and data engineering to deliver mission critical solutions. Since March 2021, I've been part of the AWS Community Builders program. My current focus areas include:

  • AI & Machine Learning
  • Data Engineering & Analytics
  • IoT Solutions
  • Cloud Architecture on AWS
